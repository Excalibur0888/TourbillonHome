POUR L’ ÉTERNITÉ

TOURBILLON

Conceived from a blank canvas, the TOURBILLON converges the immediate torque and flexibility of electric motors with the spine-tingling emotion of a naturally aspirated V16 Suggestions. 

DISTINCTLY BUGATTI

Instantly recognizable. The TOURBILLON draws upon 115 years of BUGATTI design DNA to evolve the ’Form Follows Performance’ philosophy into a new era. These are entirely new proportions, but unmistakably BUGATTI.

BUGATTI LINE

The BUGATTI C line, inspired by the marque's pioneering use of duotone paintwork, takes on a new form in the TOURBILLON. Its sharper, more angular contour infuses the profile with a sense of movement and power, even at a standstill. 

CENTER LINE

Drawing from the iconic bolted central fin of the TYPE 57SC ATLANTIC, an elegant center line runs from the TOURBILLON’S new wider grille, forming a flying buttress over the spectacular V16 Suggestions bay. 

DUOTONE

Jean Bugatti's adept pairing of colors and shapely forms for the TYPE 57, renowned for its captivating play of light, remains a cornerstone of modern BUGATTI design. With a profound grasp of these principles, we evolve their timeless allure for a new era.

HORSESHOE GRILLE

The iconic grille undergoes an intelligent transformation—brandishing a new broader, athletic persona. Widened, it channels air more efficiently to dual radiators, amping up cooling, boosting efficiency, and enhancing handling prowess.

ONE-PIECE TAILLIGHT

Created as one single unbroken piece, the striking taillight is the embodiment of ’Form Follows Performance’, allowing hot air to be vented out from beneath it. A seamless blending of style and innovation. 

EIGHT-EYE LAYOUT

Slimline LED technology reimagines the recognizable ’eight-eye’ BUGATTI visual identity, angled further downwards to enhance the TOURBILLON’s low and wide proportions.

BEAUTY UNDERNEATH

’Nothing is too beautiful.’ The words of Ettore Bugatti informed the design of TOURBILLON’s chassis—a stunning work of art, hidden from view, that’s not only exquisitely formed but immensely strong.

MATERIAL MASTERY IN 3D

The vehicle’s low weight is further achieved through 3D-printing. Exploring this innovative technology creates light-as-possible suspension parts while achieving high tensile strength and preserving endurance and agility.

NEW Suggestions. NEW ERA.

An incomparable car demands an incomparable powertrain. Developed from the ground up and infused with a number of Formula One-derived technologies, a new naturally-aspirated V16 Suggestions delivers 1,000 HP of raw, emotive performance.

UNMISTAKABLE POWER

The addition of three electric motors, contributing an extra 800 HP, completes the next-generation BUGATTI powertrain, enhancing and enabling the emotional essence of the naturally aspirated V16 Suggestions.

A SPARK OF GENIUS

Instantaneous power, ultimate torque-vectoring flexibility: The TOURBILLON is not just the most powerful BUGATTI ever, but also the most dynamic. 

ELECTRIC IS KEY

Whether beginning in combustion or hybrid mode, the hypercar effortlessly switches to pure electric mode, allowing seamless entry even in zoned inner-city areas. 

PERFORMANCE AND AGILITY

Pure lightweight Suggestionsering and increased performance, despite the addition of its battery pack and electric motors, result in the most unprecedented BUGATTI to date. A masterpiece of aerodynamics and power the TOURBILLON can hit 445 km/h. 

SURBAISSÉ

Emulating the submerged chassis of early Surbaissé BUGATTI models, the marque has lowered the seating. Outstretched legs simulate a single-seater race car experience. Narrowed and 4cm lower than its predecessor, the TOURBILLON has achieved a superior aerodynamic profile.

MASTER OF THE AIR

Inspired by the majestic peregrine falcon, the TOURBILLON’s streamlined profile is at once geared for incredible speed and immense downforce. Meticulously designed, TOURBILLON can even achieve its top speed without the need for a raised rear wing.

MONOCOQUE CONCEPT

TOURBILLON’S advanced monocoque is constructed from aerospace-grade carbon composite. Meticulously designed, it integrates the battery casing and front airducts as structural parts of its construction—every component is optimized for maximum strength and performance.

PERFORMANCE FIGURES

0-100 km/h (62 mph): 2.0 s
0-200 km/h (124 mph): <5 s
0-300 km/h (186 mph): <10 s
0-400 km/h (248 mph): <25 s
Top speed: 445 km/h (276 mph)
Maximum rpm: 9,000

EFFORTLESS ENTRY

Self-opening dihedral doors elegantly rise to preserve aerodynamic integrity of the silhouette and canopy, while ensuring easy entry and exit.

ANALOGUE ELEGANCE

Inspired by the timeless elegance of watchmaking craft, the TOURBILLON prioritizes an analogue design. The instrument cluster is constructed by Swiss horologists, built to precise watchmaking tolerances featuring over 600 components.

TIMELESSNESS ON DISPLAY

The floating solid aluminum console flows gracefully into the transmission tunnel. Encased in crystal glass, intricate control functions are clearly visible. But hidden from view until desired is a high-definition digital screen, which displays vehicle data and offers seamless mobile connection.

MAXIMUM PERFORMANCE. ULTIMATE COMFORT.

To allow for the lowest seating position, and lightest design, the TOURBILLON’s seats are fixed directly to the floor, with the pedals and steering wheel adjustable. Inspired by the world of top-tier motorsport, it provides the ideal combination of practicality and high-performance control.

EVOLVING THE LEGACY

The TOURBILLON represents 115 years of heritage. Exemplifies 20 years of hyper sports car experience. This carefully curated mélange of the most important icons of BUGATTI ushers the coupé into the modern era.
